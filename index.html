Chrome Extension

Privacy Policy

JD Resume Assistant  ·  Last updated: March 2025

Short version: JD Resume Assistant does not collect, store, or transmit any of your data to any server we operate. Everything stays on your device.

1. What This Extension Does

JD Resume Assistant is a Chrome extension that helps job seekers automatically fill out job application forms and generate tailored resumes. It works by:

  • Reading the text content of the job listing page you are currently viewing
  • Detecting form fields on job application pages
  • Opening ChatGPT or Claude (your choice) in a new tab and sending a structured prompt
  • Waiting for the AI to finish, extracting the response, and filling your form fields automatically

2. Data We Collect

We do not operate any servers and do not collect any data remotely. The only data stored is saved locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage, and includes:

  • Your profile (optional) — name, email address, phone number, LinkedIn URL, GitHub/portfolio URL, city/location, notice period, expected salary, and a short professional summary. You enter this voluntarily in the extension settings to personalize form auto-fill answers.
  • AI platform preference — whether you prefer ChatGPT or Claude. This is a single setting stored locally.

This data never leaves your device except when it is included in the prompt sent directly to the AI platform you choose (ChatGPT or Claude) in your own browser session.

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect browsing history or track which websites you visit
  • We do not use analytics, telemetry, or crash reporting tools
  • We do not collect authentication information, passwords, or credentials
  • We do not collect health, financial, or payment information
  • We do not read your emails, messages, or personal communications
  • We do not sell, rent, or share any data with third parties

4. How We Use the Job Page Content

When you click "Start Session", the extension reads the visible text content of the active browser tab (the job listing or application form you are on). This text is processed entirely within your browser and sent as part of a prompt directly to the AI platform you have selected (ChatGPT at chatgpt.com or Claude at claude.ai) in a new tab — exactly as if you had manually copied and pasted it yourself.

We do not store, log, or transmit this page content to any server we control.

5. Third-Party AI Platforms

When you use this extension, a prompt containing the job description and your profile information is sent to the AI platform you select:

  • ChatGPT — operated by OpenAI. Their privacy policy applies: openai.com/policies/privacy-policy
  • Claude — operated by Anthropic. Their privacy policy applies: anthropic.com/privacy

You are responsible for reviewing the privacy policies of these platforms. The extension uses your existing logged-in session on these platforms — it does not create accounts or authenticate on your behalf.

6. Permissions Used

  • activeTab — to read the job description and form fields from the page you are currently viewing, only when you explicitly click the extension
  • scripting — to inject a status sidebar into the job page and a response-detection script into the AI platform tab
  • storage — to save your AI platform preference and optional profile locally on your device
  • tabs — to open the AI platform in a new tab and switch back to your job page after the AI finishes
  • Host permissions (chatgpt.com, claude.ai, all_urls) — to inject the response watcher into AI platform tabs, and to read and fill form fields across any job board or employer career page

7. Data Retention

Your profile and preferences are stored locally using Chrome's chrome.storage API and remain on your device until you clear them via the extension settings or uninstall the extension. Uninstalling the extension removes all locally stored data.

8. Children's Privacy

This extension is intended for job seekers and is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the extension after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
